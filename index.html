What myhealth offers

Everything for Better Health

A complete health companion — from symptom checking to daily tracking, medicine reminders, and personalised AI advice.

🧠

AI Symptom Diagnosis

Describe your symptoms and get an instant AI-powered diagnosis with medicine recommendations and dosage schedule.

📅

Daily Health Tracker

Log vitals every day — heart rate, blood pressure, blood sugar, temperature — and see your health trend with visual charts.

💊

Medicine Schedule

AI generates your precise medicine plan with morning, afternoon, and night timing, food instructions, and duration.

✅

Daily Task Suggestions

Get personalised daily health tasks based on your profile — exercise, hydration, sleep, diet — and tick them off as you go.

🔒

Stay Logged In Always

Your account stays active. No repeated logins — just open and see your health dashboard instantly, every time.

📊

Health Score & Reports

Your personalised health score updates daily based on your vitals, tasks completed, and medicine adherence.
